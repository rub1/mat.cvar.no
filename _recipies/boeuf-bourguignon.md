## Recipe

- 1,5 Tbsp olive oil
- 150g lean bacon
- 750g beef, stew meat
- 1 large white onion
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1-2 carrots, in chunks
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 sprigs of thyme
- 2 sprigs of parsley
- 1 bottle burgundy red wine (or any Pinot Noir)
- salt & pepper to taste


- 7-8 button mushrooms, halved
- 3-4 very small onions, halved
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1 sprig of thyme 
- salt, pepper


- 500g baby potatoes, steamed

### for finishing:

- 1 sprig of thyme
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1 Tbsp butter
- finely chopped parsley
